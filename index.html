How it looks in Caruso

LIST 1Investing Entity ↔ Contact email lists

held per contact, per entity
Primary and secondary contacts are treated exactly the same — every migrated contact receives the same email settings. Contacts without an email address are not migrated, unless the entity has no email addresses at all (Group B), in which case a single placeholder-email account is created as the key account.

LIST 2Investing Entity postal list

one election per entity — five categories
Post is recorded once on the investing entity, so there is a single postal election and a single physical copy of each mailing — no duplicate letters. Marketing is a post-only category here.

LIST 3Contact email lists

held on the person, across all their entities
Portal Access & Compliance is locked on for everyone — password resets, security and AML/KYC notices cannot be turned off. Marketing email is off for every contact at migration; Group A entities receive marketing by post instead, and investors can be subscribed by your team at any time.
